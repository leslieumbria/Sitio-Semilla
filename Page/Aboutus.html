About Us

We are a creative agency based in Baja, specialized in branding strategies and the development of visual communication systems. We play as a part of our clients project or brands to create solutions that truly connect with their audiences. Semilla means seed in Spanish (our native language). We sow the seeds of creativity and expertise into the brand or project, and with a lot of work and passion, we reap the greatest results. That's how we do it.
